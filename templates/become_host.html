Frequently Asked Questions

It's completely free to create a host account and list your property on Tournest. We only charge a small service fee (typically 3%) when you receive a booking. There are no monthly fees or hidden charges.

You can list almost any type of property on Tournest, including apartments, houses, vacation homes, private rooms, shared rooms, unique spaces like treehouses or boats, and more. The platform is designed to accommodate a wide variety of accommodations.

Tournest processes payments securely and releases funds to hosts 24 hours after guest check-in. You can receive payments via direct deposit, PayPal, or other available payment methods in your region. You can set up and manage your payment preferences in your host dashboard.

Tournest provides 24/7 customer support, a host protection insurance program, verified guest profiles, secure payment processing, and tools to help you manage your listings effectively. We also offer resources and tips to help you become a successful host and maximize your earnings.
