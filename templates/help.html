Frequently Asked Questions

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of the page. You'll need to provide your name, email address, phone number, and create a password. You can choose to sign up as a guest or a host. Once you've filled in all the required information, click "Sign Up" to create your account.

Tournest accepts various payment methods including major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay. The available payment methods may vary depending on your country or region. All payments are processed securely through our platform to protect both guests and hosts.

To cancel a reservation, go to your account and navigate to "Trips" or "Bookings." Find the reservation you want to cancel and click on "Cancel Reservation." The refund amount will depend on the host's cancellation policy, which is displayed on the listing page and in your booking confirmation. Some bookings may be non-refundable or have partial refunds depending on how close to the check-in date you cancel.

To become a host, click on "Become a Host" in the navigation menu. You'll need to create an account if you don't already have one. Then, you'll be guided through the process of creating your listing, which includes providing details about your space, setting house rules, adding photos, and setting your price. Once your listing is complete, you can publish it and start receiving booking requests.

Yes, Tournest takes data security very seriously. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal and payment information. We only share your information with hosts or guests as necessary to facilitate bookings. You can review our Privacy Policy for more details on how we collect, use, and protect your data. If you have specific concerns about your account security, you can contact our support team.
